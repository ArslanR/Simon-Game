Press A Key to Start

Instructions:

After you start the game, the computer presses a button.

You have to press the same button and remember the previous pressed buttons as the level increases.

You need to remember the pressed button sequence, if the sequence is correct you go to the next level and so on.

For example, level 1: computer presses green, you press green.

level 2: computer presses yellow, you press green and then yellow to proceed to level 3.
